# Pawsome - AI Cat Health Assistant

# App Overview
Pawsome is an AI-powered mobile application designed to help cat owners manage their pets’ health, daily care routines, and veterinary information in one organized platform. The application improves pet care management by allowing users to maintain cat profiles, record daily activities, monitor health indicators, and receive intelligent guidance through an AI chatbot.

# Problem Statment
Cat owners often store vaccination records, vet details, and daily care information in different places, making it difficult to track their pet’s health properly.
Common issues include:
1. Forgetting daily pet care tasks
2. Difficulty identifying serious symptoms
3. Delayed or unnecessary vet visits
4. Lack of organized health records
Pawsome solves these problems by providing a centralized digital assistant for cat care.

# Target Users
1. Cat owners (single or multiple cats)
2. Busy pet owners
3. New pet guardians seeking guidance
4. Users needing organized pet health tracking

# Features
1. It allows cat owners to create and update the profile of their cats.
2. It provides them the feature to log their daily activities and maintains a care log.
3. It calculates the health and stress level for cats based on their daily condition, appetite, bowel movement, urination, and weight.
4. It includes an AI chat bot that allows users to add care records, and provides them with care tips based on the symptoms of cats.
5. This app also keeps record of health checkups of cats including the doctor they visited and their last consultation date, along with their vaccination and allergies records.
6. The app also finds nearest vets and recommends them to the owners.
